About Reejan Limited

Reejan Limited is a Gangmasters & Labour Abuse Authority (GLAA) licensed labour provider and UK registered company based in London. We supply skilled and dependable workers across food processing, packaging, agriculture and logistics. Our mission is to connect the right people with the right opportunities while ensuring full compliance, fair treatment and strong worker welfare.
